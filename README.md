# probabilistic-shaping-for-nonlinear-fiber-optics
MATLAB simulation codes for reproducing the numerical results of the ICEE 2022 paper “On the Impact of Probabilistic Shaping on Fiber Nonlinearities,” including EGN-model-based evaluation of SCI, XCI, MCI, NLI, effective SNR, and SER for probabilistically shaped PM-16QAM WDM fiber-optic systems.
